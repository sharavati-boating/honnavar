Common Questions

What should we bring?

Just your vibe! Plus sunglasses, a hat, sunscreen, and your phone/camera. We provide premium, sanitized life jackets for everyone.

What about bad weather?

Your safety is our obsession. If the weather looks grumpy, we offer a full refund or a hassle-free reschedule. You never lose money with us.

How do we pay?

Super easy. We accept UPI (GPay/PhonePe) or Cash. For large groups, a tiny advance secures your golden hour slot.
